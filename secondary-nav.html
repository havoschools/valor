Valor Hiring Profile

We are excited to build an outstanding faculty at Valor—a community of professionals, learners, and friends who are united around Valor's mission and vision. As charter schools, we hire people from a wide variety of backgrounds, including those with teaching degrees and certifications, as well as those who do not have these credentials but who have pursued other formative life experiences. We are seeking passionate teachers and professionals who embody these qualities:

  • Mission and educational vision alignment
  • Subject matter expertise
  • Strength of character
  • Love of learning
  • Commitment to professional growth and development
  • Humility and receptivity to coaching
  • Practical wisdom
  • Aptitude for working with students
  • Classroom leadership capacity
  • Commitment to collaboration and team unity
  • Strong work ethic and an enthusiastic, positive attitude
Employee Benefits

Valor offers employees a competitive benefits package, including health benefits and participation in the Teacher Retirement System of Texas (TRS) for eligible positions. We offer competitive plans for medical, dental, vision, accident, disability, and life insurance.

For questions about careers at Valor, please email careers@valoreducation.org.
